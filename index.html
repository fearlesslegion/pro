About Us

Fearless Legion is a crew founded by Nova/Leonis. We are worldwide with 4 divisions: Inferno, Main, Deity, and more coming soon! Our community is loyal, fun, and welcoming to all players. Join us for tournaments, giveaways, and an amazing experience!

We believe in creating a friendly and supportive environment where everyone can grow and enjoy their gaming journey. Whether you're here for competitive play or just to meet new friends, we have something for everyone. Our divisions are designed to suit different playing styles, and our crew members are always ready to help each other out.

Rules

  • Fighting within the crew is strictly prohibited.
  • Promoting other Discord servers is not allowed.
  • Trading or selling accounts is prohibited (trading channel coming soon).
  • No spamming in any channel.
  • Report toxic behavior to the founder or captain.

Our rules are simple, but important. Following these rules ensures that everyone can enjoy their time in the Fearless Legion without any unnecessary conflicts. We encourage respect, sportsmanship, and teamwork. If you ever feel unsure about something, don't hesitate to ask for clarification!
